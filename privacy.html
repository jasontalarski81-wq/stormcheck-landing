Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Trustman Roofing LLC ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our storm check intake form at stormchecktrustmanroofing.com.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit our quote form, we collect: your name, mobile phone number, property address, email (optional), and any notes you provide. We also collect the timestamp, page URL, and user agent at the time of consent for compliance purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information solely to:

  • Schedule and provide your free roof inspection
  • Send SMS and email communications you have consented to receive
  • Provide insurance claim assistance and project updates
  • Respond to your inquiries

3. SMS Communications

By providing your mobile number and checking the SMS consent box, you agree to receive text messages from Trustman Roofing including inspection scheduling, project updates, and storm alerts. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance. SMS consent is not a condition of purchase.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We may share information only with: (a) service providers who help us operate (e.g., SMS delivery via Twilio, hosting), strictly under confidentiality; (b) your insurance carrier when working on your claim, with your permission; (c) law enforcement when legally required.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS) and secure storage.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at (636) 541-7005.
